Who we are?

We are KitKat, a beloved confectionery brand renowned worldwide for our iconic chocolate-covered wafer bars.

As part of the Nestlé family, we take pride in our rich heritage dating back to 1935 when our founder, Rowntree's of York, first introduced the delectable combination of crispy wafer and smooth chocolate. Over the decades, we've become synonymous with breaks, offering a delightful indulgence for moments of relaxation and enjoyment. Our commitment to quality and innovation has allowed us to continually evolve, introducing new flavors, formats, and initiatives to satisfy the cravings of our loyal fans while staying true to our timeless recipe. Embracing the spirit of breaks, we strive to bring joy and comfort to people's lives, one delicious KitKat at a time.

What are our products?

KitKat Chunky, featuring a thicker, chunkier version of our classic bar, and limited edition.

KitKat offers a diverse range of products to cater to various tastes and preferences, ensuring there's a KitKat for everyone to enjoy. Our classic offerings include the original milk chocolate-covered wafer bar, characterized by its distinctive crispy layers and smooth chocolate coating. Additionally, we provide a variety of flavors and variations, such as dark chocolate, white chocolate, matcha green tea, strawberry, and many more, to tantalize taste buds and satisfy cravings.

Why choose us?

We rely on two things - quality and taste. That should answer your question!
